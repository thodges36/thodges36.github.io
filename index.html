About Me

Ted is learning to be a full-stack web devleoper in the KU Coding Boot Camp. Currently, he is a marketing manager at Saint Luke's Health System. He has experience in copywriting, graphic design, and project management.

Ted graduated from the University of Notre Dame with a Bachelor of Art in English. He earned his Master of Business Administration at Kansas Wesleyan University. At Notre Dame, he fenced for the 2011 NCAA Nationship Championship team.

In his personal life, Ted volunteers at the Midwest Transplant Network, enjoys (sometimes) watching the Chiefs, Royals, and Jayhawks, and spends time with his family and friends.
